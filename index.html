The Dublin School of Drums

Established in 1979, TDSD was set up to provide a high level of drum tuition specialising in drum-kit performance. Our teachers are renowned on Irish and foreign music scenes for their high level of musicianship and for the diverse and numerous acts they have toured and recorded with. Also, the school regularly hosts workshops and lessons with guest teachers.

Included in your enrollment at the school is access to our exclusive lessons page. Here the student will find regularly added videos ranging from basic videos on rudiments to higher concept videos such as extended soloing in a jazz context.
